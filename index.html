Why El Paso?

El Paso, border town of Texas

El Paso TX is a city that borders both Mexico and New Mexico. Its name means 'the pass' or 'the step'. It is an army based city and is home to more than 600,000 people. The city has one of the largest Hispanic populations in the US.

The El Paso area transitions between both hot and cold desert climate. With record breaking hot summers and can also get a few inches of snow each year.
